Mentor Spotlight: Very Rev Fr.Benjamin

Very Rev.Fr.Benjamin has been a guiding light for our students and youth. His dedication to inclusive education and cultural healing has transformed many lives across villages. He mentors girls in tailoring, boys in Parai Isai, and supports elders with compassion and care.

His message: "Empowerment begins with listening. Every child, every widow, every elder has a story worth honoring."
