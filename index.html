Welcome to Life Empowerment Trust - "Transformation is imminent"

Life Empowerment Trust is a charitable trust dedicated to serving marginalized communities. We believe in equal opportunity for all, regardless of caste, creed, language, or religion. Through weekend tuition, nutritious meals, and skill-building programs, we empower children, widows, and elders to live with dignity and hope.
