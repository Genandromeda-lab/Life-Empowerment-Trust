Who We Are

Life Empowerment Trust is a Charitable Trust registered under the Indian Trust Act, 1961 with the District Registrar of Acharapakkam under Reg No. 16/2024, dated June 26, 2024. Founded and directed by Juliyes Vanathaian, the Trust is committed to serving underprivileged communities irrespective of caste, colour, creed, language, or religion.

All donations to Life Empowerment Trust are Tax-Exempt Under Section 80G.

"LIFE EMPOWERMENT TRUST IS A LIFELINE FOR THOSE WHO ARE AT THE FRINGES OF OUR SOCIETY"

Why We Exist

We are solely dedicated to children who are marginalized and underprivileged‚Äîespecially those living in rural villages without access to quality education. Since 2009, Life Empowerment Centre has offered hope, opportunity, and awareness of the right to education. These children are often the first generation in their families to attend school. Our goal is to help them graduate with pride and become breadwinners for their families.

About Founder ‚Äì Juliyes Vanathaian

Juliyes Vanathaian, the founder and director of Life Empowerment Trust, was born into a Dhobi family in Then Nerkunam, Tindivanam Taluk, Tamil Nadu. Her parents washed clothes for Dalit families, often receiving food as wages. As a child, Juliyes accompanied her mother at night to beg for food and helped wash clothes at the village pond.

In school, she faced severe discrimination and humiliation from both students and teachers. The trauma led to debilitating migraines, forcing her to discontinue studies in 8th grade. But Juliyes never gave up. She learned tailoring, which restored her self-esteem. Her blouse stitching was so exceptional that even dominant caste families sought her work.

She began teaching tailoring free of cost to young girls and conducted evening study classes for children of all castes. Her personal journey of resilience and compassion inspired her to launch a weekend study centre in 2009. Today, it serves students, youth, and families from 25 villages‚Äîoffering education, counseling, and economic development.

Her message: "Every child deserves dignity, education, and opportunity. Together, we can build a more inclusive and compassionate world‚Äîwhere no one is left behind."
